## Description

The draft-to-ready mission review asks one practical question: can a worker and independent validator understand the target state from the Outcome without private chat history. It does not require planner-authored validation scenarios or evidence checklists.

## Outcome

Mission readiness review is focused on target-state clarity. A mission moves from draft toward ready only when its `Outcome` is clear enough for a worker and an independent validator to understand finished without private chat history.
