Naming refinement: avoid a 'bulk' command group. Infrastructure tools center the reviewable delta: Terraform has plan/apply, CloudFormation has change sets, Pulumi has preview/up, and kubectl has diff/apply. For Atelier, prefer a command group around a one-shot change/change-set/graph delta rather than volume. Candidate direction: 'atelier change preview <file>' and 'atelier change apply <file> --yes', with no persistent desired-state reconciliation.
