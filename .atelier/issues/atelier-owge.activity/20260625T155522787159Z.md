Design decision: semantic style tokens should remain Atelier-owned. Prefer wrapping anstyle behind StylePolicy rather than exposing raw color-library calls or extension traits in panel or command code. Required tokens include heading, secondary/context, success/ready/pass, warning, danger/blocked/fail, and active/in-progress; colorless output must keep all meaning in text.
