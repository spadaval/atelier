Support explicit typed links through a preferred generic `atelier link add/remove/list` surface. Link types include blocks, blocked_by, depends_on, related, duplicates, supersedes, derived_from, part_of, implements, validates, evidenced_by, and planned_by. Do not overload dependencies to represent hierarchy.

## Acceptance

Typed links are persisted, shown, exported, rebuilt, and linted; dependency aliases remain reserved for true sequencing; `atelier link` supersedes `issue relate/unrelate/related` as the preferred public surface while preserving compatibility where needed; invalid or unknown relation behavior is documented and tested.
